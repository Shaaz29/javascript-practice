Q4. Print the factorial of a number n.
(Factorial of a number n is the product of all positive integers less than or equal to the given positive integer and is denoted by n!)

Examples:
7! = 1 × 2 × 3 × 4 × 5 × 6 × 7 = 5040
5! = 1 × 2 × 3 × 4 × 5 = 120
3! = 1 × 2 × 3 = 6
0! = 1
